Careers

Small teams. Senior people. Disproportionate leverage.

FrostAura is designed for leaders, principal engineers, senior researchers, and operators who can own systems end to end. We hire for taste, judgment, technical depth, and calm execution.

How we work

We prefer compact teams with exceptional range.

FrostAura teams stay intentionally lean. Each senior person should create outsized leverage: setting direction, building real systems, raising standards, and unlocking future capability across more than one company.

Leadership tracks

Senior engineering tracks

Research and field tracks
